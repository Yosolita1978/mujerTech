Module 1: Generative AI 101

Demystifying Artificial Intelligence

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes

ü§ñ What is Generative Artificial Intelligence?

Generative AI consists of models that learn patterns from massive datasets to create new content: text, images, audio. Think of it as your personal creative assistant.

‚úÖ What it CAN do

  • Generate creative ideas
  • Create content drafts
  • Summarize information
  • Translate texts

‚ùå What it CANNOT do

  • Think for itself
  • Replace your judgment
  • Guarantee 100% accuracy
  • Work without supervision

Module 2: Effective Prompting

The art of giving instructions to AI

‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes

üìù Structure of a Good AI Message (Prompt)

The CTFT Formula:

C
Context: Who you are and what role AI assumes
T
Task: Specific action you need
F
Format: How you want the response
T
Tone: Desired communication style

üõ†Ô∏è Complete Prompt Example

Context: You are a marketing advisor for women entrepreneurs in Latin America

Task: I need to create 3 Instagram posts about natural cosmetics

Format: List with emojis, maximum 150 characters each

Tone: Friendly, motivating and professional

üí° Best Practices

  • Be specific in your instructions
  • Provide examples when possible
  • Review and adjust the responses
  • Iterate: improve your prompt based on results
  • Verify the generated information

üìã Formula for Image Prompts

Basic structure:

[Image type] + [Specific product] + [Visual style] + [Complementary elements] + [Lighting] + [Colors] + [Quality]

Example:
"Product photography of natural aloe vera facial cream in elegant glass jar, minimalist style, white background, surrounded by fresh aloe leaves and water drops, soft natural lighting, green and white tones, high resolution, professional quality, 8k"
üí° Pro Tip: Always add "high quality, professional photography, 8k" at the end for better results.

üéØ Image Prompt Builder

Complete these fields to create your prompt:

  1. What product do you sell? ___________________________
  2. What style do you want? ‚ñ° Minimalist ‚ñ° Lifestyle ‚ñ° Professional ‚ñ° Flat Lay ‚ñ° Mockup
  3. What additional elements? ___________________________
  4. What dominant colors? ___________________________

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too vague prompts: "A cream" ‚Üí "Hydrating facial cream in amber glass container"
  • Forgetting context: Specify "product photography" or "advertising image"
  • Not mentioning quality: Always include "high quality, professional"
  • Ignoring the brand: If you have a logo, mention "with space for logo"
  • Not iterating: Generate 3-5 versions and choose the best one
